The problem

Every October, India
sets fire to a goldmine

Farmers burn Parali because they have no fast, affordable alternative. It poisons the air, wastes resources, and leaves farmers with nothing. AagniChain changes that.

🌾

No market access for farmers

Farmers lack buyers, logistics, and pricing transparency. Burning is the cheapest exit they know.

🏭

Businesses lack sustainable feedstock

Paper mills, bioenergy plants, and packaging companies need biomass but can't source it reliably from rural Punjab and Haryana.

🌧

India pays the health price

Delhi's AQI crosses 450 every November. Millions suffer respiratory illness. The cost is borne by everyone.

How it works

Three steps from
field to factory

AagniChain is a transparent marketplace and logistics layer that makes Parali trade as simple as ordering online.

01

Farmer registers

A farmer lists available Parali quantity, location, and preferred pickup window. No middlemen, no paperwork maze.

02

Business discovers & bids

Verified businesses browse available lots, place transparent bids, and agree on fair prices — creating real competition for farmers.

03

We coordinate collection, baling, transport, and payment — farmers get paid on time, businesses get reliable supply.
